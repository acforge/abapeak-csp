How it works

Three steps. Under two minutes from first field to finished draft.

  1. 1
    Fill in the session summary
    Enter your client alias, the date, session duration, what happened, and what you’re planning next. No free-text narrative — structured fields only.
  2. 2
    Client data is scrubbed on your device
    Before anything is sent, AbaPeak replaces any detected identifiers with placeholders. What leaves your device is already de-identified.
  3. 3
    Review, finalize, copy to your EHR
    A complete DAP-format first draft is ready in seconds. You review it, correct anything that’s off, and paste it into your EHR. The clinical judgment stays yours.

Common questions

Is AbaPeak HIPAA-compliant?
AbaPeak is not a HIPAA-covered entity and does not function as a Business Associate. The system is designed so that client identifiers are replaced with placeholders on your device before any request is transmitted — our servers never receive readable client information. You are responsible for using client aliases (not real names) in all fields.
What note types does AbaPeak support?
AbaPeak generates DAP-format notes for CPT codes 97155-HN (Protocol Modification) and 97156-HN (Caregiver Training), for both BCBAs and BCaBAs.
Where are my notes stored?
Notes are saved only in your browser’s local storage on your own device. They are not stored on our servers. Clearing your browser data or switching devices will remove your note history.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes. Monthly and annual subscriptions can be cancelled any time through your Lemon Squeezy account. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
What browsers does AbaPeak support?
AbaPeak works on Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari on desktop and mobile. Chrome or Edge on Windows/Mac is recommended for the best experience. Some native date and time pickers behave differently across browsers — typed entry is always supported as a fallback.
