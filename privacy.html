Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. Who We Are

AbaPeak is a web-based tool that helps Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and Board Certified Assistant Behavior Analysts (BCaBAs) generate structured ABA session notes in DAP format for CPT codes 97155-HN (Protocol Modification) and 97156-HN (Caregiver Training).

AbaPeak is operated by IT Integrated Solutions LLC. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at hello@abapeak.com.

2. This Service Is Not a HIPAA-Covered Entity

AbaPeak is not a HIPAA-covered entity and does not function as a Business Associate under HIPAA. AbaPeak does not receive, transmit, or store Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA. The system is designed so that client identifiers are replaced with placeholders on your device before any request is transmitted to our servers.

You remain responsible for how you use AbaPeak in your clinical practice and for ensuring your documentation practices comply with applicable laws, regulations, and your organization's policies.

3. What Information We Collect

a. Email address

If you join our waitlist, we collect your email address to send you product updates. Waitlist signups are routed through Formspree (see §7) to our inbox. If you purchase a subscription, Lemon Squeezy collects your email at checkout and we use it to communicate trial status, billing, and subscription details.

b. Payment information

Subscription payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy, which serves as the Merchant of Record. AbaPeak does not collect, store, or have access to your payment card details. Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy governs payment data.

c. Usage analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics service. Plausible collects no personal identifiers, uses no cookies, and does not track individuals across sites. We use it to understand aggregate usage patterns (page views, general geography, device type).

d. License key

When you enter your license key, it is converted to a non-reversible hash and stored locally on your device. The hashed value is transmitted to our servers only to verify that you hold a valid license. The raw license key is never stored on our servers.

4. How AbaPeak Handles Session Data

AbaPeak's architecture is designed to minimize the clinical information that leaves your device:

  • You enter session information using a client alias (e.g., "Client-07"), not a real client name or identifier.
  • Before any request is transmitted, a built-in scrubber runs entirely on your device. It identifies and replaces any detected client identifiers with placeholders.
  • What is transmitted to generate a note is already de-identified. Our servers and our AI provider receive only the scrubbed, placeholder-containing content — not your original input.
  • Generated notes are not stored on our servers. Notes are saved only in your browser's local storage (IndexedDB) on your own device.

5. What We Do Not Collect

AbaPeak does not collect:

  • Real client names, dates of birth, Social Security numbers, or other personal identifiers
  • ICD-10 codes, diagnostic information, clinical notes, or session content in readable form
  • Any Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined under HIPAA
  • Behavioral session recordings (screen replays, heatmaps, or similar tracking tools)

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold about you, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS), hashed license key storage, and edge-infrastructure security via Cloudflare. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

7. Third-Party Services

AbaPeak uses the following third-party services:

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing and license management (Merchant of Record). Privacy Policy
  • Plausible Analytics — aggregate, cookieless usage analytics. No personal identifiers collected. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — web hosting, edge network, and DNS. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic — AI model provider for note generation. Receives only the scrubbed, de-identified request from your device. Privacy Policy
  • Formspree — waitlist and contact form submission routing. When you join our waitlist or contact us via our support form, your email address and message are transmitted through Formspree to our inbox. Privacy Policy

8. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you have an active subscription or open trial. You may request deletion at any time by emailing hello@abapeak.com and we will respond within 30 days. Generated notes and session data are stored only on your device and can be cleared from within the app at any time.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. Contact us at hello@abapeak.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

AbaPeak is intended for licensed clinical professionals and is not designed for, marketed to, or knowingly used by individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has provided personal information through our service, contact us immediately.

The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of AbaPeak after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
